Jupiter Facts And Figures:

The length of a day on Jupiter is 9hr 55min.

Jupiter's average distance from the Sun is 778.4 million km.

The surface gravity of Jupiter is 2.36 times greater than Earth.

A Jovian year is 11.86 Earth years.

Jupiter has a mass 318 times that of Earth.

Jupiter has a diameter of 142,984 km.
